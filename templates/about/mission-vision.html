Mission & Vision



Mission

The mission of Buddha Jyoti Laghubhitta is to create income and generate self-employment and to improve the socio economic environment through micro credit of the rural poor people at their doorsteps to reduce poverty level. लघुवित्त सेवाको माध्यमबाट ग्रामीण विपन्न परिवारलाई स्वरोजगारको अवसर श्रृजना गरी गरीवी घटाउने Mission रहेको छ ।



Vision

The vision of Buddha Jyoti Laghubhitta is to uplift the socio-economic Status of the rural poor people through micro finance services and participatory self-development of the Nepal. लघुवित्तको माध्यमबाट नेपाल राज्यभरिका ग्रामीण विपन्नहरुको सामाजिक तथा आर्थिक अवस्था उकास्ने यस संस्थाको Vision रहेकोछ ।
